Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gaper Inc. ("Gaper," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our AI marketing platform (the "Platform"), including when you connect a third-party social media account such as TikTok to schedule and publish content through the Platform.

By connecting your TikTok account to Gaper, you authorize us to access and process certain information from your TikTok account through TikTok's official APIs, solely to provide the features you request. You can disconnect your account and request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information you provide when registering, such as name, email address, and password.
  • Connected-account data obtained from third-party platforms you link (see Section 2).
  • Content you submit for scheduling or publishing, including videos, images, captions, and metadata.
  • Usage and device data, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps, collected automatically via cookies and similar technologies.

2. TikTok Data We Access

When you connect your TikTok account through TikTok's OAuth flow, you grant us permission to access specific information through TikTok's APIs. Depending on the scopes you authorize, this may include:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., display name, avatar, and open ID) so we can show which account is connected.
  • Access and refresh tokens that allow the Platform to act on your behalf for the permissions you granted.
  • Video and post information needed to upload, publish, and report on content you initiate through the Platform.

We only request the minimum permissions needed for the features you use, and we access TikTok data only to perform actions you initiate. Our use of information received from TikTok APIs adheres to the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and related policies.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: connecting your accounts, scheduling, and publishing content at your direction.
  • To display analytics and the status of content you publish.
  • To authenticate you, maintain your account, and provide customer support.
  • To secure the Platform, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use TikTok data for advertising profiling or any purpose unrelated to providing the Platform.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the Platform:

  • Service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, database, and infrastructure vendors) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • The platforms you connect (e.g., TikTok), to carry out the publishing actions you request.
  • Legal and safety disclosures when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.

5. Storage, Security & Retention

We store access tokens and personal data using industry-standard safeguards, including encryption of credentials at rest and in transit, access controls, and monitoring. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

We retain connected-account data and tokens only for as long as your account remains connected and as needed to provide the Platform. When you disconnect a TikTok account or close your Gaper account, we delete the associated tokens and connected-account data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. You may also opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in any such message.

7. Disconnecting & Deleting Your Data

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time from within the Platform's settings, or by revoking access in your TikTok account settings. Disconnecting immediately stops our access and triggers deletion of the related tokens and connected-account data. To request full deletion of your Gaper account and associated data, email us at privacy@gaper.io and we will process your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for businesses and users aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here and update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated through the Platform or by email where appropriate.
